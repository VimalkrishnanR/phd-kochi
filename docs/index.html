'The whole PhD system to me is an abomination. I don't have a PhD myself, I feel myself very lucky I didn't have to go through it. I think it's a gross distortion of the educational process...I think it is an evil system and it has ruined many lives', said Freeman Dyson, the reenowned physicist and mathematician, in an interview recorded years back. 'Shodhganga', is an open online database that gives access to thousands of PhD theses submitted at various Indian universities. Shodhganga's website says it is a 'reservoir of Indian theses' and the homepage proclaims a milestone of uploading the, '6,00,000th thesis' last year. A stopwatch style counter on Shodhganga's homepage, displays the number of theses and synopses uploaded, the number of contributing universities, and more. I wonder what Dyson would have made of this ever expanding and incorporeal tower of babel!
